# RadioPrep 🩺

A web-based educational and reference platform built for diagnostic radiologic technologists and healthcare trainees. RadioPrep simplifies studying radiological examination protocols, reviewing positioning guides, and sharing insights with peers.

---

##  Getting Started

### Access the Platform

👉 [Live Deployed App Link](https://radioprep-website.onrender.com)

---

##  How to Use RadioPrep

1. **Create an Account:** Sign up as a regular trainee or check the **"Are you part of our team?"** box and enter the staff passkey for administrative access.
2. **Explore Protocols:** Browse detailed examination cards covering positioning, views, and radiological parameters.
3. **Save & Review:** Easily navigate through reference materials to prepare for clinical rotations and exams.
4. **Engage & Comment:** Ask questions or post notes on examination cards. Track all your posted comments from your personal dashboard.
5. **Staff Management:** Authorized staff can create, update, and manage protocol cards to keep references up to date.
